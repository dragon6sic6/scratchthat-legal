Scratch That

Privacy Policy

Scratch That collects nothing. There is no account, no login, no analytics, no tracking, and no ads. We have no servers, and we never see what you scratch or send.

No data reaches us

The app has no backend. The lines you’ve recently seen are stored only on your own device, so the app doesn’t repeat itself. Nothing is uploaded to us, ever.

Messages you send

Cards you send travel through Apple’s iMessage, exactly like any other message. We are not involved in their delivery and cannot read them.

Photos

The app only touches a photo if you explicitly choose one to share, using the system share sheet. Nothing is uploaded to us.

No third parties

We share nothing with anyone, because we collect nothing in the first place.
